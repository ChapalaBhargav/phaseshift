About Digital Hunt

Digital Hunt is an exciting online treasure hunt where participants solve clues, navigate challenges, and race against time. Whether you're a puzzle enthusiast or an adventure seeker, this event is designed to test your skills and creativity.

Rules of the Hunt

  • Participants must solve each clue to unlock the next level.
  • All entries must be submitted online through the official portal.
  • Time is of the essence! Each clue comes with a timer.
  • Collaboration is encouraged, but each participant must submit their own solutions.
  • Be respectful and have fun!
